Frequently Asked Quetion

Can I play my PS4 games on my PS5 console?

+

Yes. PS5 consoles are backwards compatible with PS4 games, with over 4000 titles ready to play. Find out more here: PS4 games playable on PS5 consoles.

When did PS5 consoles come out?

+

PS5 consoles launched in select territories on 12 November 2020, with a full global launch soon after on 19 November 2020.

Can I use Discord on PS5 consoles?

+

Yes, you can link your account for PlayStation Network and your Discord account to share your gaming activity, use voice chat and more. Get full details here: How to link your Discord account to PlayStation Network.

How much do PS5 consoles cost?

+

PS5 consoles are available in a range of digital and disc-based editions, as well as with different controller options and as software bundles. Check online retailers for availability and price details.

Players in the US, UK, Germany, Austria, France, Luxembourg, Belgium, Netherlands, Spain, Portugal and Italy can shop direct with PlayStation and enjoy additional benefits including free delivery on every order, from direct.playstation.com.

Where can I get help and support for my PS5 console?

+

Visit PlayStation.com/support/ for assistance with a range of console, subscription and game issues.

Is the slim PS5 console the same as the original model?

+

The same features that make PS5 the best place to play have been packed into a smaller form factor, with a 1TB SSD for more internal storage. The new PS5 has been reduced in volume by more than 30%, and weight by 18% and 24% compared to the previous models.
